🎬 Back to the Future Trilogy: Summary

🚗 Overview

📅 Back to the Future (1985)

Marty McFly accidentally travels from 1985 to 1955 in a DeLorean time machine built by Doc Brown. While stuck in the past, he must ensure his teenage parents fall in love—or risk erasing his own existence. With time running out, Marty seeks Doc’s help to get back... to the future.

⏩ Back to the Future Part II (1989)

Marty and Doc travel to the year 2015 to prevent a future disaster involving Marty’s children. But when a sports almanac falls into the wrong hands, they return to a drastically altered 1985. The only fix? A trip back to 1955, without running into their past selves.

🐎 Back to the Future Part III (1990)

Marty races to 1885 to rescue Doc, who’s trapped in the Wild West and marked for death. With no fuel for the DeLorean and time running out, they must improvise their way home—cowboy style. Love, action, and steam-powered ingenuity close out the trilogy.
